Valley DevFest 2019 and GDG Fresno in partnership with 59 Days of Code and Code for Fresno are holding a 23hr hackathon!

Register here: {{ 'Home.HackathonRegistration' | translate:lang }}

While you compete in the Hackathon you are welcome to Valley DevFest 2019; there will be a light breakfast, lunch by LaJacka, talks and workshops (a great chance to ask experienced developers for advice).

The teams will choose one of two prompts; the Design Challenge or the Technical Challenge.

Design Challenge:
  • Create a user-friendly “Know Your Rights” site, page, or graphic about criminal record relief. This can be a website or even a digital image for social media.
Technical Challenge:
  • Create a services usability review site, which evaluates the services available to people seeking criminal record relief (i.e., expungement, sealing, dismissal, reduction, etc).

Each prompt will have a prize of $500 and a year of membership to The Hashtag!
